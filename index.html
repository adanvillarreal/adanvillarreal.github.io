Software Engineer Intern at Google

Summer 2018 | San Francisco, CA

I worked in the Firestore's Watch Team. My project consisted on optimizing the way queries with limits and offsets are processed. The optimization was focused in a scenario similar to the one of a scoreboard in which there is a great number of entities' additions and changes and the client just needs a small subset of the results (e.g. the top 10). This lead to a decrease on filter latencies and a cleaner codebase. This was implemented mainly in C++. The load tests used for benchmarking were written in Java.

Among the different processes that I integrated where the download of utterances, annotation of entities and the upload of annotations to the AI. This project was implmented using Ruby on Rails and consuming APIs from LUIS and Elastic Search.
